Alternate Website

If you have an already existing profile page that you would like to be used for your CHIP.org profile put the url here. Any CHIP.org links to you will redirect to that page.

Publications

Publications must be in BibTex format. Anything else will cause your profile page to render incorrectly.
To get BibTex format out of EndNote: Export your publications list as BibTex. Paste the resulting text here.
Example:
@article{11,
authors = {LastName, B. and lastName2, R. T.},
title = {An Excellent example Article Name},
journal = {Test Journal Monthly},
volume = {7},
pages = {93},
pumedid = {1234546787},
year = {2006},
url = {http://testjounalmonthly.org/2006/7-93}
}

Status

  • Draft: Incomplete profile, will not show on the frontend. When you first fill out your profile, even a little, you should change this to Published.
  • Published: Published and available on the frontend
